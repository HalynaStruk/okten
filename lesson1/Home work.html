Simpsons family

The Simpson family consists of fictional characters featured in the animated television series The Simpsons.

The Simpsons are a nuclear family consisting of

  • Homer
  • Marge
  • Bart
  • Lisa
  • Maggie

Homer

Homer Jay Simpson is the bumbling husband of Marge, and father to Bart, Lisa and Maggie Simpson. He is the son of Mona and Abraham "Grampa" Simpson.

Marge

Marge Simpson is married to Homer and mother of Bart, Lisa and Maggie Simpson. She was raised by her parents, Jacqueline and Clancy Bouvier. She has a pair of sisters, the joyless Patty and Selma, both of whom vocally disapprove of Homer.

Bart

Bartholomew JoJo Simpson is a fictional character in the American animated television series The Simpsons and part of the Simpson family.Bart's hobbies include skateboarding, watching television, reading comic books, playing video games and generally causing mischief.

Lisa

Lisa Marie Simpson is a fictional character in the animated television series The Simpsons. She is the middle child and most accomplished of the Simpson family.

Maggie

Margaret Evelyn Lenny "Maggie" Simpson is a fictional character in the animated television series The Simpsons and a part of the Simpson family, notably the youngest member.
